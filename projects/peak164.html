Challenge:

The challenge for this project was to create two separate design strategies based on one existing marketing communications challenge. This was solution two. The second ice cream food truck strategy was developed to create a stronger, storytelling brand that would better connect with a millennial target audience.

Peak 164 is an ice cream truck brand that offers culturally inspired ice cream flavours. The brand blends imagery of ice cream with destinations around the world to suggest an experience of travel and adventure. Social media, in particular Instagram, would be used to support the brand messaging and create a unique brand experience for a target audience nicknamed the “Carmen Sandiegos” — Millennial explorers and adventurers.

Response:

With a series of unique ice cream flavours such as Everest and Zhangye, the brand identity allows the audience to imagine themselves tasting the destination in which the flavours originate.
