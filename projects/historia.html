Challenge:

The challenge for this project was to create two separate design strategies based on one existing marketing communications challenge. This was solution one.

Historia is an ice cream food truck that aims to break through Vancouver’s highly saturated ice cream market with a nostalgic-focused brand strategy. Research showed that the target audience’s shared interest in the history of previous generations was a relevant strategy to capture interest.

Response:

Historia offers a variety of uniquely named ice cream flavours such as the Moon Landing and Atomic Bomb. The brand concept is designed to connect the target audience to recognizable eras from the past. The brand’s identity replicates an authentic 60s experience with a retro colour palette and a bold script typeface, giving the audience a taste of the past.
